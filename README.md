#  Bangalore Restaurant Market Analysis
## End-to-End Data Analytics Case Study using SQL, Python & Power BI



##  Project Overview

The Bangalore restaurant industry is highly competitive, making data-driven decision-making essential for successful business expansion.

This project analyzes **51,155 restaurant listings** from Bangalore to understand customer engagement, market competition, pricing patterns, cuisine demand, and service features that influence restaurant performance.

The project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow—from business understanding to actionable business recommendations—using SQL, Python, and Power BI.



#  Business Problem

A restaurant group plans to expand into Bangalore but lacks sufficient market intelligence to make informed business decisions.

Management needs answers to questions such as:

- Which locations offer the best market opportunities?
- Which cuisines have the highest demand?
- How does pricing influence customer engagement?
- Do online ordering and table booking improve customer interaction?
- Which market segments should new restaurants target?

Without analytical insights, expansion decisions may result in poor location selection, ineffective pricing strategies, and increased competitive risk.



#  Business Objectives

The project aims to answer the following business questions:

- Identify locations with the highest restaurant concentration.
- Measure customer engagement across different locations.
- Analyze the relationship between pricing and customer engagement.
- Evaluate the impact of online ordering and table booking.
- Identify dominant cuisine categories.
- Generate strategic business recommendations for restaurant expansion.

#  Key Findings

### Market Competition

BTM contains the highest number of restaurant listings, indicating intense market competition.

### Customer Engagement

Lavelle Road records the highest median customer engagement despite having fewer restaurant listings.

### Pricing

Premium restaurants demonstrate the highest median customer engagement.

### Online Ordering

Restaurants offering online ordering are associated with higher customer engagement.

### Table Booking

Restaurants supporting table booking also exhibit stronger customer engagement.

### Cuisine Trends

North Indian cuisine dominates the Bangalore restaurant market in terms of restaurant listings.


#  Business Strategy Recommendations

## 1. Expansion Strategy

Evaluate both restaurant density and customer engagement before selecting expansion locations. High competition does not necessarily indicate stronger customer demand.



## 2. Pricing Strategy

Premium restaurants showed higher typical customer engagement.

Businesses should align pricing with their target customer segment while maintaining strong value through food quality, ambience, and service.



## 3. Digital Service Strategy

Restaurants offering online ordering demonstrated higher customer engagement.

Investing in reliable online ordering platforms can improve accessibility and customer reach.

---

## 4. Reservation Strategy

Restaurants providing table booking also showed higher customer engagement.

Reservation systems may improve customer experience, particularly for premium dining establishments.



## 5. Cuisine Strategy

North Indian cuisine has strong market demand but also significant competition.

Businesses should evaluate market saturation and consider opportunities for differentiation or underserved cuisine segments.



## 6. Data-Driven Decision Making

Expansion decisions should combine multiple indicators—including location performance, customer engagement, pricing, cuisine demand, and service features—rather than relying on a single metric.
